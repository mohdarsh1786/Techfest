Virtual reality


  (VR) is a live, direct or indirect, view of a physical, real-world environment whose elements
are augmented by computer-generated sensory input such as sound, video, graphics or GPS
data. During the workshop students will be introduced to basics of Reality/Augment Reality
its applications, working. Students work on the VR IDE, build modules which include interm
facing camera, identifying markers, overlaying text, image, video.
